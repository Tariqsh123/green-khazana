About Green Khazana

Green Khazana is Pakistan’s first people-owned, digitally monitored forest platform. We believe that every citizen has the power to create a greener, more sustainable future. By combining technology, community participation, and responsible forestry, we enable individuals to plant, monitor, and benefit from trees in a transparent and impactful way. Our mission goes beyond planting trees — we aim to protect the environment, empower local communities, and build long-term wealth for everyone involved. Together, we are turning green initiatives into measurable environmental, social, and economic outcomes, ensuring that Pakistan’s forests—and its people—thrive for generations to come.

Why Green Action Matters for Pakistan

Pakistan contributes just 1% to global CO₂ emissions, yet faces some of the harshest climate impacts in the world.

Average temperatures have risen over 1°C in the last few decades.

Extreme heatwaves are now three times longer than in the 1990s.

Floods, droughts, and air pollution are threatening livelihoods across the country.

Forests cover only 5% of the land, far below the global average of 31%.

Climate change is no longer just an environmental issue—it is a social, economic, and survival challenge for every Pakistani.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Green Khazana

How does Green Khazana track planted trees?

We use a combination of GPS tracking, unique QR codes, and regular updates from our field partners. Each tree planted through our platform gets a unique ID that can be tracked throughout its lifecycle.

What types of organizations can use Green Khazana?

Our platform is designed for any organization interested in environmental sustainability - including corporations, schools, universities, NGOs, government agencies, and community groups of all sizes.

How do you ensure the survival of planted trees?

We work with local experts and communities to ensure proper planting and maintenance. Our partners provide regular updates and replace any trees that don't survive, ensuring your investment has lasting impact.

Can individuals participate or only organizations?

While our primary focus is organizational management, individuals can participate through their organizations or through our community planting events. We're also developing an individual participation feature for future release.
