Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

GREEN KHAZANA is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you interact with our website, donation programs, Kidspenuership initiatives, and corporate green ownership services.

2. Information Collection

We collect personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, and payment details when users engage with our programs. Additional information may be collected automatically, including IP addresses, device information, and browsing behavior, to enhance user experience and ensure proper service delivery.

3. Use of Information

Collected information is used to manage donations, monitor tree ownership, communicate program updates, provide support, and improve GREEN KHAZANA services. It may also be used for reporting environmental impact and carbon credit tracking purposes.

4. Sharing of Information

GREEN KHAZANA does not share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Information may be shared with service providers, regulatory authorities, or partners strictly for program administration, legal compliance, or operational purposes.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. Data is stored securely, and access is restricted to authorized personnel only.

6. User Rights

Users have the right to access, correct, or delete their personal information. Requests regarding personal data can be made through official communication channels, and GREEN KHAZANA will respond in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

7. Cookies and Tracking

We may use cookies, analytics, and tracking technologies to improve website performance, monitor usage patterns, and provide a more personalized experience. Users can manage cookie preferences through their browser settings.

8. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites or services. GREEN KHAZANA is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of external sites. Users are encouraged to review the privacy policies of any third-party websites they access.

9. Updates to Policy

GREEN KHAZANA reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Users will be notified of significant changes, and continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the revised policy. We encourage users to review this policy periodically.

10. Contact Information

For questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or the handling of personal information, please contact GREEN KHAZANA through our official channels. We are committed to addressing user concerns promptly and maintaining transparency in all privacy-related matters.
